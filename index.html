Features
Everything you need to budget

Ledger covers the full picture — from logging a coffee purchase to tracking a six-month savings goal.

Income and expense tracking

Log every transaction — income or expense — with a category, amount, date, and optional notes. Edit or delete anything at any time.

Monthly budgets by category

Set a spending limit for each category every month. See progress bars showing how close you are to each limit and what you have left.

Remaining spendable amount

Your dashboard shows exactly how much money you can still spend this month, calculated against your total budget and what you have already spent.

Charts and spending trends

Three chart views: income vs expenses month by month, spending broken down by category, and your daily spend trend for any period you choose.

Daily, monthly, and yearly views

Filter your data by any time period — current month, last three months, full year, or a custom range. Compare periods to spot patterns in your spending.

Savings goals

Create savings goals with a target amount and date. Track progress manually and see at a glance whether you are on track to hit each goal in time.

Custom categories

Add your own spending and income categories on top of the 18 built-in defaults. Assign colors to make your charts easy to read at a glance.

Private and secure

Your financial data is stored in a private database with row-level security. No one else can read your transactions. You can delete your account at any time.
